### Questions -> Otázky:
- Idem ja? -> Do I go?
- Ideš ty? -> Do you go?
- Ide on? -> Does he go?
- Ide ona? -> Does she go?
- Ideme my? -> Do we go?
- Idete vy? -> Do you go?
- Idú oni? -> Do they go?

### Present Simple (go) -> Jednoduchý prítomný čas (ísť):
- Ja idem -> I go
- Ty ideš -> You go
- On ide -> He goes
- Ona ide -> She goes
- My ideme -> We go
- Vy idete -> You go
- Oni idú -> They go

### Present Simple (do) -> Jednoduchý prítomný čas (robiť):
- Ja robím -> I do
- Ty robíš -> You do
- On robí -> He does
- Ona robí -> She does
- To robí -> It does
- My robíme -> We do
- Vy robíte -> You do
- Oni robia -> They do

### Negatives -> Zápory:
- Ja nejdem -> I don't go
- Ty nejdeš -> You don't go
- On nejde -> He doesn't go
- Ona nejde -> She doesn't go
- To nejde -> It doesn't go
- My nejdeme -> We don't go
- Vy nejdete -> You don't go
- Oni nejdú -> They don't go

### Past Simple -> Minulý čas (ísť):
- Ja som išiel -> I went
- Ty si išiel -> You went
- On išiel -> He went
- Ona išla -> She went
- To išlo -> It went
- My sme išli -> We went
- Vy ste išli -> You went
- Oni išli -> They went
